Goal of this project: Bookmark Search Assistant

Organize how information is presented to users to create a better framework for how we search.

Create a Chrome extension to search through your archives in your bookmark, which tend to be websites or online resources one wishes to return to, visited beto remind users about that information before moving on to the next points of search and results.

Current Problem

  • Search results may vary even with identical search terms.
  • Search time varies for finding relevant information.
  • Information overload.
  • Bookmarks are out of sight

Relevant Reseaerch: Search Through Bookmarks

Users commonly report problems in refinding important websites. To address this, some create Bookmarks (also called web Favorites) to improve the refinding of these websites. Previous research notes that these Bookmarks are rarely used. However, these prior studies did not systematically observe and quantify the frequency and success of Bookmark retrievals, or compare their success with other retrieval methods. The results suggest that unless the Bookmarks were highly visible, the participants did not use them. Furthermore, for websites that the users had visited, bookmarked websites were not better retrieved than those that had not been bookmarked.
